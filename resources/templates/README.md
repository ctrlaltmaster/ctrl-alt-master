# Templates

This folder contains reusable templates created or curated by the Ctrl+Alt+Master community to support learning, practice, and organization.

Templates are designed to help people structure their work, reflect on progress, and build sustainable technical habits.

---

## What belongs here

Templates may include:
- Study or practice planners
- CTF or lab tracking sheets
- Reflection or debrief frameworks
- Checklists for setup, review, or reset
- Organizational systems for learning workflows

Templates should be practical, reusable, and clearly structured.

---

## What templates are not

- Long-form guides or explanations
- Personal journals or walkthroughs
- One-off notes without reuse value
- Commercial products or paid offerings

Templates are meant to support learning, not replace it.

---

## Format

Templates may be hosted outside GitHub (Canva, Google Docs, PDFs, etc.) and linked here once finalized.

Each template should include:
- A clear name
- A short description of its purpose
- Instructions for use (brief)
- A link to the template
