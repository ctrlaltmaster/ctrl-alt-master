# Code of Conduct

Ctrl+Alt+Master is a community built on curiosity, respect, and shared learning.

We welcome people from all backgrounds, experience levels, and identities. Participation in this space means agreeing to uphold the values outlined below.

---

## Our Standards

We expect all community members to:

- Be respectful and considerate in communication
- Engage in good faith and assume positive intent
- Welcome beginners and avoid gatekeeping
- Give credit where it is due
- Respect differing perspectives and experiences

Harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary behavior will not be tolerated.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include, but are not limited to:

- Harassment or intimidation
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Personal attacks or insults
- Sharing private information without consent
- Disruptive behavior that undermines the community

---

## Enforcement

Community organizers reserve the right to remove content, restrict participation, or take other appropriate action if this Code of Conduct is violated.

Enforcement decisions are made with the goal of maintaining a safe and constructive learning environment.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all Ctrl+Alt+Master spaces, including but not limited to:
- GitHub repositories
- Community forums or chat platforms
- Events and meetups
- Social media interactions connected to the project

---

## Questions or Concerns

If you have questions about this Code of Conduct or need to report an issue, please reach out to a project organizer.
